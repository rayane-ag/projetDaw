Welcome to the Graduation Platform

A platform that connects students and professors to manage graduation projects efficiently.

About Us

Our platform simplifies the graduation process by enabling students to choose topics, connect with professors, and manage their projects seamlessly. Professors can add topics, monitor student progress, and guide their research efforts, ensuring an efficient graduation experience.

For Students

Students can explore various graduation project topics, select their preferences, and connect with professors for guidance and project management.

For Professors

Professors can create and manage project topics, guide students throughout their research, and monitor project progress easily.
